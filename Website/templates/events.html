Down Your Local

Join the last 'Newport Museum's Down Your Local' session at The Murenger pub on Tuesday, 6 December at 3pm. Disappearing Newport; a chat about buildings that have gone (and a few that still remain). Newport Museum's 'Down Your Local' sessions are informal discussions about local history and archaeology. There is nothing to join, nothing to commit to and nothing to pay. Why not drop in and see what it's all about?
